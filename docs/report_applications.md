# IoMT Applications in Healthcare Monitoring

## Advanced Wearable Biosensors
- Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGMs) provide minute-by-minute glucose readings.
- Clinical-grade ECG patches can monitor cardiac rhythm for weeks and detect arrhythmias.

## Ingestible Sensors and Smart Implants
- Smart pills track medication adherence and send activation signals to wearable patches.
- Connected pacemakers become networked health nodes, reporting fluid and rhythm data daily.

## Remote Patient Monitoring and Hospital-at-Home
- Smart beds use pressure sensors to prevent bedsores and detect movement.
- Ambient Assisted Living systems monitor routine activity and notify caregivers when patterns change.

## AI and Edge Computing
- Predictive analytics combine sensor and environmental data to anticipate medical events.
- Edge computing enables immediate decisions locally, reducing latency and enabling safe, offline operation.
