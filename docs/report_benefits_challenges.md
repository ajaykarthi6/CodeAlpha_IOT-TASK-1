# Benefits and Challenges of IoMT

## Benefits
- Clinical Outcomes: Early detection through continuous tracking reduces ER visits and readmissions.
- Patient Experience: Home-based monitoring improves comfort, autonomy, and mental well-being.
- Economic Efficiency: Automation reduces nurse workload and decreases inpatient care costs.

## Challenges
- Cybersecurity: Medical devices require end-to-end encryption and zero-trust architecture.
- Data Privacy: Sensitive health information must comply with HIPAA, GDPR, and patient control policies.
- Interoperability: Standardization is needed so devices from different manufacturers can work together.
- Power Constraints: Long-term monitoring demands low-power, self-sustaining sensor designs.
